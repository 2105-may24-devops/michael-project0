set title "Homemade Bread"

add ingredient yeast 0.25 oz
add ingredient sugar 9.5 tsp
add ingredient water 2.25 cup
add ingredient salt 1 tbsp
add ingredient flour 6.5 cup
add ingredient "canola oil" 2 tbsp

add step "In a large bowl, dissolve yeast and 1/2 teaspoon sugar in warm water; let stand until bubbles form on surface"
add step Whisk together remaining 3 tablespoons sugar, salt and 3 cups flour.
add step Stir oil into yeast mixture; pour into flour mixture and beat until smooth. Stir in enough remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, to form a soft dough. 

add step Turn onto a floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, 8-10 minutes. 
add step Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top.
add step Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, 1-1/2 to 2 hours.

add step Punch dough down. Turn onto a lightly floured surface; divide dough in half. Shape each into a loaf. Place in 2 greased 9x5-in. loaf pans. Cover and let rise until doubled, 1 to 1-1/2 hours.

add step Bake at 375° until golden brown and bread sounds hollow when tapped or has reached an internal temperature of 200°, 30-35 minutes. Remove from pans to wire racks to cool.
